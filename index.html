My Clifton StrengthsFinder Top 5 Results:

These StrengthsFinder results are a fantastic description of how I operate. I am happiest existing in my mind, always looking to collect and digest all kinds of new information and skills - learning has always been a lifelong passion for me, whether teaching myself to knit, learning about (and practicing) the fascinating biomechanics of lifting heavy things, or picking up a new language (human OR software!), and I am thrilled to be entering a field where perpetual learning is not only encouraged, but required in order to stay relevant.

Done List App

Deployed App / Repo

This application was my personal capstone project at Prime Digital Academy. I had approximately 2 weeks to conceive of an idea for an application, and then build it from scratch.


"Done List" is a "reverse to-do list" which allows the user to track what they've done each day. I've found in my own life that tracking what I've done often helps me accurately evaluate how I've actually been using my time, and how to better direct my goals and productivity.
My goals in the near future for this app are to make it mobile-friendly, to add "delete" and "edit" functions in case of a mis-entry, and to allow more granular tracking of accomplishmnts, such as being able to check the last time a task occurred, or to show charts with a graphic display of accomplisments over time.
