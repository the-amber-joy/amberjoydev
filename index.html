About Me


My Clifton StrengthsFinder Top 5 Results:

  1. Learner
    2. People strong in the Learner theme have a great desire to learn and want to continuously improve. In particular, the process of learning, rather than the outcome, excites them.

  2. Input
    3. People strong in the Input theme have a craving to know more. Often they like to collect and archive all kinds of information.

  3. Intellection
    4. People strong in the Intellection theme are characterized by their intellectual activity. They are introspective and appreciate intellectual discussions.

  4. Relator
    5. People who are strong in the Relator theme enjoy close relationships with others. They find deep satisfaction in working hard with friends to achieve a goal.

  5. Deliberative
    6. People strong in the Deliberative theme are best described by the serious care they take in making decisions or choices. They anticipate the obstacles.

These StrengthsFinder results are a fantastic description of how I operate. I am happiest existing in my mind, always looking to collect and digest all kinds of new information and skills - learning has always been a lifelong passion for me, whether teaching myself to knit, learning about (and practicing) the fascinating biomechanics of lifting heavy things, or picking up a new language (human OR software!), and I am thrilled to be entering a field where perpetual learning is not only encouraged, but required in order to stay relevant.

I worked very briefly as a field archaeologist right out of college, then decided to transition into office jobs where the weather didn't affect my ability to work. But in my own time, I've always been a technophile, and found myself feeling very at-home with the software development team I supported at Symantec Corporation (now Veritas Technologies). However, I was always interested in getting "behind the code" someday, and am very excited to be making that move now.
